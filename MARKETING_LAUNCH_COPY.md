## FAQ

### Where can I download Pantry & Plate?

Pantry & Plate is available on the App Store:
https://apps.apple.com/us/app/pantry-plate/id6761082174

Google Play link coming soon.

### Is Pantry & Plate free?

Eligible new users can start with a 7-day free trial. An active subscription is required after trial.

### What subscriptions are offered?

Pantry & Plate offers Premium Monthly and Premium Annual subscription options.

### What does Pantry & Plate help with?

It helps with pantry tracking, recipe saving and organization, My Cookbook, meal planning, smart grocery lists, household-friendly organization, optional connected shopping, and AI-assisted recipe and image tools.

### Does Pantry & Plate guarantee savings?

No. Pantry & Plate is designed to help you plan around what you have and what is missing, but it does not guarantee savings.

### Does Pantry & Plate provide medical, allergy, calorie, or weight-loss guidance?

No. Pantry & Plate is not a medical, allergy-safety, calorie-tracking, or weight-loss planning service.

### Does connected shopping mean checkout happens inside Pantry & Plate?

No. Optional connected shopping may help with handoff when you choose to use it, but checkout and order handling are managed by the connected grocery provider.

### How do I manage or cancel a subscription?

Manage or cancel subscriptions through the applicable App Store or Google Play account settings. Deleting a Pantry & Plate account does not automatically cancel a store subscription.
