How it works

From pantry to plate in one weekly flow.

Instead of planning in one app, checking your pantry in another, and rewriting a grocery list by hand, Pantry & Plate keeps the loop connected.

Save recipes you will actually cook

Build a personal cookbook from recipes your household already likes, imported links, or new ideas you want to try.

Track what is already at home

Keep pantry staples, product details, and expiration timing visible before you plan or shop.

Plan meals with pantry context

Drop meals into the week and see what is covered while you plan, not after the list is already made.

Shop only the gap

Turn planned meals into a focused shopping list that prioritizes what is missing instead of what you already own.

Cook without losing your place

Open recipe details and guided cooking flows from the same system that planned the meal.

What makes it different

Built for the part of cooking most apps skip.

Pantry-aware before checkout

Most meal-planning tools create a list first. Pantry & Plate is designed to compare your plan against what is already in your kitchen.

Private household recipes

Family recipes can be personal. Pantry & Plate is designed around private household recipe storage and explicit sharing.

One connected weekly system

Home, Pantry, Cookbook, Plan, and Shop are designed to roll into one another instead of becoming separate chores.

Trust

Your recipes stay yours.

Private household recipes are intended to be visible only to you and your household members. Pantry & Plate does not sell, publish, or use private household recipes for marketing or advertising.

AI recipe extraction and generation run only when you choose to use those features. Camera and photo access are used only for user-started actions such as barcode scanning, recipe capture, or selected uploads.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Pantry & Plate available yet?

Not yet. Pantry & Plate is in active development as we prepare for release. You can request beta access or launch updates by emailing support@pantryandplate.app.

How do I request beta access?

Use the Request beta access button or email support@pantryandplate.app with the subject line "Pantry & Plate Beta Access Request."

Will there be App Store or Google Play links?

We will add official download links when the app is ready for public release.

Does Pantry & Plate use AI?

Pantry & Plate may use AI for user-initiated recipe extraction or generation. That means prompts, links, or images are processed only when you choose to use those features.

Are private recipes shared publicly?

No. Private household recipes are intended for you and your household members. Any public sharing should be explicit, opt-in, and clearly labeled.

Can Pantry & Plate help reduce duplicate grocery purchases?

That is one of the main goals. The app is designed to compare planned meals against pantry context so shopping lists focus on missing items.
