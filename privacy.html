Privacy Policy

How Perfect Pantry handles your information

Last updated: February 6, 2026

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Perfect Pantry website at perfectpantryapp.com and the Perfect Pantry mobile app. It explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices users have.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information such as name and email address.
  • Pantry, recipe, meal planning, and shopping list content you create.
  • Support messages you send through email or contact forms.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and app metadata (platform, OS version, app version).
  • Operational logs and diagnostics needed for reliability and debugging.
  • Basic website usage signals needed for security and performance.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain pantry, recipe, plan, and shopping workflows.
  • Support account access, recovery, and household sync behavior.
  • Respond to support requests and product feedback.
  • Monitor reliability, prevent abuse, and improve product quality.
  • Comply with legal obligations where required.

4. Private Household Recipes

We understand that family recipes can be cherished, guarded, and meaningful history. If you trust Perfect Pantry with a private recipe, we honor that trust.

  • Private recipes are intended to be visible only to you and your household members.
  • We do not sell, publish, or share private household recipes with other households.
  • We do not use private household recipes for marketing or advertising.
  • Any public sharing of a recipe must be explicit, opt-in, and clearly labeled.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers required to run the app and site (for example, infrastructure, authentication, storage, and support tooling), and when required by law.

6. Data Storage and Retention

Data is retained for as long as needed to provide the service, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention windows may vary by data type and operational need.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. No internet transmission or storage method is guaranteed to be perfectly secure.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account data.
  • You may contact us about objections or restrictions related to processing.
  • You may unsubscribe from launch or product communications at any time.

Submit requests to support@perfectpantryapp.com.

9. Account Deletion Requests

You can delete your account from within the app. If you choose to delete your account from within the app, all account data is wiped from the local device and from Perfect Pantry servers. You can also request account deletion by emailing support@perfectpantryapp.com with subject line Account Deletion Request from the account email.

10. Children

Perfect Pantry is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy over time. If changes are material, we will update the effective date and provide additional notice where appropriate.

12. Contact

Privacy and data requests: support@perfectpantryapp.com
