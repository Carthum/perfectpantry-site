1. Overview

Perfect Pantry (“Perfect Pantry,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website PerfectPantryApp.com and the Perfect Pantry mobile application (collectively, the “Services”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

A) Information you provide

  • Account information such as name and email address (and login credentials, if applicable).
  • App content you add or create, such as saved recipes, meal plans, pantry items, quantities, categories, notes, and expiration dates.
  • Support communications (messages, screenshots, and other information you send us).

B) Information collected automatically

  • Device and app information (device type, operating system, app version).
  • Usage information (screens visited, features used, crash logs, performance data).
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (typically city/region level), if applicable.

C) Cookies and similar technologies (website)

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to operate and understand how it is used. You may be able to control cookies through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain the Services
  • Save and sync your pantry, meal plan, and recipe data
  • Send reminders and notifications you request (e.g., expiration alerts)
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Respond to support requests
  • Detect, prevent, and address security or technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the Services (e.g., hosting, analytics, email delivery, customer support tools).
  • Legal and safety authorities if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers if we’re involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide the Services and for legitimate business purposes (such as compliance, dispute resolution, and enforcement).
