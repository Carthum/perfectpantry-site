FAQ

Common support questions

Can I subscribe from this website?

No. The website currently does not handle memberships or payments.

I reported a bug. What happens next?

We triage reports by impact and reproducibility. If we need more context, we will follow up by email.

Where should I request a feature?

Use the support form and choose Feature request so it lands in the product feedback queue.

How do I request account deletion?

Send an email to support with subject Account Deletion Request. Full policy details are listed on the privacy page.
