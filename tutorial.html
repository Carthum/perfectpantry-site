Quick start (2 minutes)

Use this section for the fastest path to a “successful first session.”

  1. Create your account (or sign in).
  2. Add 5 pantry staples you always keep (ex: rice, pasta, eggs).
  3. Save 2 recipes you want to make this week.
  4. Add 1 meal to your plan and generate your shopping list.

Troubleshooting

Keep this short and link to Support for anything deeper.

I didn’t get a reminder notification.

Check notification permissions on your phone. In the app, confirm reminders are enabled in Settings.

My data didn’t sync.

Make sure you’re signed into the same account and have an internet connection. If it persists, contact support.

I found a bug.

Please contact support and include your device type, app version, and screenshots if possible.
